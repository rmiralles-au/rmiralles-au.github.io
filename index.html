Supply Chain 05

RUTA — Lead Time Lookup Assistant

Lead time questions at La Cuisine meant either interrupting the one person in Supply Chain who maintained a complex multi-tab Excel model, or navigating it yourself across 40 suppliers, 9 destination markets, and 5 different route types. I built RUTA, a conversational AI assistant that answers any lead time question in plain language — returning a complete stage-by-stage breakdown plus supplier-specific operational context like incoterms, MOQs, and real vs. quoted production times. The knowledge base is script-generated directly from the source Excel so it stays accurate when the data changes.

~40 suppliers across 9 destination markets

Sales Operations 06

Inventory to Sales Matching Automation

A colleague needed to enrich a 395-line retail inventory report with salesperson and order data that only existed in a separate sales export. There was no shared key, item numbers were stored inconsistently as text, integers, and floats across both files, and the relationship was one-to-many — meaning a standard VLOOKUP would silently return wrong results by grabbing only the first match. I built an automation that normalizes identifiers across both files, resolves every one-to-many match correctly, and outputs the enriched report with the original formatting fully preserved.

99% match rate across 395 items and 2,912 records
2 hours saved per report cycle

Python  ·  openpyxl
